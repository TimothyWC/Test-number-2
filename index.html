Our Story

Two people. One standard. No shortcuts.

Cornerstone is run by Justin and Marisa — a family operation, not a call center. Every project we take on gets our eyes on it, our hands on it, and our name attached to it. That's the whole business model.

We've been laying stone in Vaughan long enough to know that good work shows up in the details no one photographs: the base prep under the pavers, the drainage behind the wall, the edge that sits flush five winters later. We build like we're going to drive past it for the next twenty years — because we are.

— Justin & Marisa
Owners, Cornerstone Interlock & Landscape Design
